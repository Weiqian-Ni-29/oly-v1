Our Story in Motion

Company History

2005

Company Founded

Oliya was established in Shanghai Songjiang High-Tech Park, focusing on precision screen printing technology R&D.

2008

First Automated Production Line

Introduced first fully automated screen printing line, increasing production capacity by 300% with precision reaching ±0.01mm.

2012

International Certification

Obtained ISO 9001 quality management certification, began exporting to European and American markets.

2016

Technological Breakthrough

Developed nano-level precision printing technology, successfully applied in medical device field.

2020

New Factory Operation

New Songjiang factory began operation with total area of 15,000 sqm, annual capacity increased to 5 million units.

2023

Digital Transformation

Fully implemented smart manufacturing system, achieving digital and visual production processes.
